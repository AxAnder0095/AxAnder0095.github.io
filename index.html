Projects

Fail-stack Program

This is a still in works program that would simulate the armor and weapon enhancing system from the MMO I play, Black Desert Online. The way this program works is you have a piece of gear that you upgrade to it's last enhancement level. The levels go as, 1-15 and once you reach level 15, you reach the second part of the enhancing system. From here on out there are only 5 levels left, PRI, DUO, TRI, TET and PEN. Each enhancement level you go up, the percentage to enhance to the next level drastically drops. From PRI to DUO the enhancement change could be around 55% which is not bad, but when you try to go from TET to PEN, the very last level, the enhancement chance will drop to around 6%. If you fail an enhancement chance, it will drop to it's previous level.
